Baguette

Crispy on the outside, soft and airy on the inside — the traditional French baguette is a staple of every good bakery. This small-batch recipe gives you a warm, fresh baguette perfect for one, without needing a big oven or fancy tools.

Save Rate Share
Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 Mins 4 hrs 40 mins 5 hrs
Serving Yield
1 2-3 mini Croissant

Ingredients

  • 100 g bread flour (¾ cup)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  • 60 ml warm water (4 tbsp)

Direction

  • Step 1

    Make the Dough Mix all ingredients until smooth. Let rest 1 hour.

  • Step 2

    First Rise Fold dough gently, then let rise again for 30–45 minutes.

  • Step 3

    Shape Shape into a mini baguette (long oval). Let rest 30 mins.

  • Step 4

    Score and Bake Preheat oven to 220°C (428°F). Score the top with a sharp knife, spray some water inside oven (for steam). Bake 20–25 minutes until golden and crispy.
