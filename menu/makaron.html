Makaron

Light, crisp shells with a chewy center — macarons are the perfect bite-sized treat. This simplified small-batch version lets you make just a few, without wasting ingredients or spending hours in the kitchen.

Save Rate Share
Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 Mins 4 hrs 40 mins 5 hrs
Serving Yield
1 2-3 mini Croissant

Ingredients

  • 30 g powdered sugar
  • 30 g almond flour
  • 25 g egg white (about 1 small egg white)
  • 20 g granulated sugar
  • (Optional) food coloring
  • 1 tbsp softened butter
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • Few drops of vanilla extract or flavoring

Direction

  • Step 1

    Make Shells Whisk egg white until foamy. Add sugar gradually, beat to stiff peaks. Sift in almond flour + powdered sugar. Fold until smooth and flows like lava.

  • Step 2

    Pipe and Rest Pipe small circles on parchment. Tap tray to remove bubbles. Let sit 30–60 mins until dry to the touch.

  • Step 3

    Bake Bake at 150°C (300°F) for 12–15 mins. Let cool.

  • Step 4

    Fill Mix buttercream. Pipe onto one shell, sandwich with another.
